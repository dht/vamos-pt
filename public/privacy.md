# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

## General

VAMOS ("we", "us" or "VAMOS") operates a website for discovering events, places and activities (the "Site"). This policy explains what information is collected when you use the Site and how we use it.

## Information We Collect

The Site does not include user accounts or forms for submitting personal details, and we do not ask you to provide a name, email address, telephone number, CV or similar personal information.

We use Google Analytics through Firebase Analytics to obtain statistical information about use of the Site. This may automatically collect information such as:

- pages viewed, visit times and duration of use;
- device type, operating system, browser, screen size and the website or link from which you arrived;
- approximate geographical location derived from your IP address;
- actions on the Site, such as viewing an event, selecting a filter, switching between list and map views, marking interest in an event, adding an event to a calendar or sharing a link.

Google Analytics may use cookies and online identifiers to distinguish between users and visits. We do not send names, email addresses, telephone numbers or other direct personal identifiers to Google Analytics.

## How We Use Information

We use statistical information to understand how people use the Site, measure use of features and content, identify problems and improve the Site. VAMOS does not use this information to create user accounts, receive job applications or pass CVs to third parties.

## Local Storage on Your Device

The Site may store preferences and usage state in your browser, such as filters, view settings, events you have marked and scroll position. This information is used to operate the Site and retain your preferences on your device. You can remove it by clearing the Site's browser data.

## Google and External Services

Information collected through Google Analytics is processed by Google in accordance with its terms and privacy policy. The retention period for Analytics data is determined by the service settings and Google's rules.

The Site also uses Google Maps in its map view. Opening the map may cause your browser to connect to Google and transmit technical information in accordance with Google's policy.

The Site contains links to third-party websites and services, such as event organisers, ticketing services, maps and social networks. Your use of those services is subject to the privacy policies of the relevant third parties, and we are not responsible for how they collect or use information.

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [Google's information about Google Analytics data retention](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245).

## Your Choices

You can block or delete cookies using your browser settings. You can also use the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout). Blocking Analytics should not prevent basic browsing of the Site, but blocking local storage may prevent preferences from being retained between visits.

## Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the Site and information relating to its operation. However, it is not possible to guarantee the absolute security of information transmitted or stored online.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The most recent update date will appear at the top of this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us using the contact details shown on the Site. Because we do not maintain user accounts and do not send direct personal identifiers to Google Analytics, we may be unable to associate Analytics data with a particular individual.
